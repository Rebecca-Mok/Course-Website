Running Flask via PyCharm

Mar 2, 2020 at 1:28pm

Folks,
In lecture, I made use of command line on installing and running Flask on my computer. Using command line can be overwhelming, if you have't used it before. So here is another approach on using Flask on your computer that may be easier.

  1. Install PyCharm on your computer. https://www.jetbrains.com/pycharm/
    As a student, you have free access to the professional edition of PyCharm.
  2. Once you have installed PyCharm on your computer, you can then follow the instructions here:
    https://www.jetbrains.com/help/pycharm/creating-flask-project.html

You will notice that in the second link, it gives you a nice step by step instructions in creating a Flask project and writing code in PyCharm, and clicking on simple buttons to run your webserver all from within PyCharm.

thanks,
abbas
